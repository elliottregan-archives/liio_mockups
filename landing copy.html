Introducing Liio.

A performance management system designed by a team of Executive Coaches to give individuals the tools needed to achieve their goals.

Liio for Individuals

Use Liio and our SPARK process to support the steps you’ll take to realize your longer-term development goals. Share your progress with others along the way, earn points for learning from taking action, and track your momentum as you grow.

Liio for Managers & Coaches

Get more involved with your employee/client development without taking up more of your time. With Liio you can support others from anywhere, share suggestions and positive feedback, and objectively track their engagement and results.

Liio for Companies

Efficiently support and measure large-scale development programs aligned with your organization’s values and direction to grow your Human Capital. Liio is designed for any level of the organization and our flexible process allows us to adapt to any new or existing development strategies.

About Us

The fundamental concept behind Liio is belief that small changes, repeated over time or by many individuals, can “snowball” to produce dramatic results. Together, through a partnership with PLS (an Executive Coaching & Leadership Development firm based in Syracuse, NY) we created Liio to apply this concept and change “what’s possible” for individuals and organizations interested in growing and developing their most valuable asset, Human Capital.

Join the Liio Project

The Liio platform is a unique concept that can add value to almost any Business, University, or Non-profit that is committed to growing and developing talent amongst their people. Our SPARK process can be applied to any number of strategies or content areas where your people need the most support, and our custom-built platform focuses on efficiency, transparency, and most importantly taking action to achieve results.
