Start Questions

The Start section is intended to make sure that you are heading in the right direction when you begin your goal. Below you’ll see a handful of questions related to this particular goal that will help lay the foundation for the progress you will make in the coming weeks and months. Complete this section by answering each of the questions provided truthfully and check back in at any point in time while you’re pursuing your goal to include new information you may have uncovered, or a change in the direction that you’ve decided on.
